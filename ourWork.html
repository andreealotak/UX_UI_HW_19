The SMKC Community Center

Our 8th Street Community Center is the heart of all of our operations and the headquarters of SMKC. Access to social workers, medical and mental health assistance are available year-round. During winter months, we’re open daily as a warming shelter to anyone in need.

If you or someone you know know needs help, please reach out. We’re here to help, no questions asked!

Services Available:

Medical Services

  • History and physical
  • Prescription assistance
  • Minor procedures
  • Case management
  • Dental

Mental Health Services

  • Diagnosis
  • General counselling
  • Trauma counselling
  • Relationship help
  • Substance abuse

Social Services

  • Veterans assistance
  • Housing assistance
  • Food assistance
  • Employment assistance
  • Documents assistance
